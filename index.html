SowGood Farms Community App

I developed this application as a resource for accessing and storing information about seasonal food crops. Users can manipulate data that are pertinent to foods they are interested in planting. The app separates the crops by season and features a list for each with foods that are suitable to plant at that time. Technologies I used include: Ruby on Rails to build the database and API, JavaScript and React.js to build an online shopping cart, and CSS for styling.

I really enjoyed the planning process for this project because I could dive in to my love of agriculture and pair it with my interest in managing data. I am currently developing improvements to create a fully functioning e-commerce application. Users will be able to create an online profile and access the flourishing online community of local growers, and growers will be able to display their food stocks and take online orders from their customers.

From small indoor kitchen pots to burgeoning home farms, this application has something for every green thumb! (click on the image to view the deployed app)
